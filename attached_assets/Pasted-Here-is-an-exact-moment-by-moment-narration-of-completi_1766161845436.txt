### **Initial Setup Description**

You begin facing a hand-drawn engineering blueprint filling the screen. At the very top are two brass funnels—**Basket 1** on the left, **Basket 2** on the right—each a wide-mouthed cone ready to receive marbles. Below them sprawls a chaotic but purposeful network:

- **Upper left region**: A tight spiral chute corkscrewing clockwise, ending at a pressure plate that controls a **diverter gate** (a small wooden drawbridge that flips between two positions).
- **Center**: A main horizontal rail running left-to-right with button-triggered **spring-loaded lifters** (tiny platforms that launch marbles upward).
- **Upper right region**: A **crossbow launcher**—a tensioned arm that smacks marbles across a gap to a separate rail network.
- **Lower region**: Three color-coded bins at the bottom—**Red Bin** (far left), **Yellow Bin** (center), **Blue Bin** (far right)—each a velvet-lined collection tray.

Your inventory at screen-top shows: **4 Red marbles**, **4 Yellow marbles**, **4 Blue marbles**.

### **Drop-by-Drop Walkthrough**

**Marble #1: Yellow into Basket 1 (Left Funnel)**
- You click and hold the **Yellow marble** in your inventory, drag it down to **Basket 1**, and release.
- It drops into the funnel, vanishes inside, then emerges at the top of the **spiral chute**.
- The marble rolls *down* the corkscrew, hugging the left wall, making a soft *clink* with each half-turn.
- It exits the spiral, landing on a **pressure plate**—a brass disk that depresses with a *thunk*.
- The plate triggers the **diverter gate** directly above it to **flip left**, closing the path to the Red Bin and opening a chute leading toward the Yellow Bin.
- The marble rolls down the now-open chute, falls three inches vertically onto the **main horizontal rail**, and rolls rightward.
- It travels smoothly, passes under a wooden arch, and drops perfectly into the **Yellow Bin** at bottom-center with a satisfying *clack*.
- **The machine is now in State 1**: The left-side diverter gate points left.

**Marble #2: Red into Basket 1**
- Drag a **Red marble** to **Basket 1** and release.
- It follows the same spiral path, hits the same pressure plate, but the diverter gate is **already left-facing**, so it stays left.
- The Red marble rolls down the chute, lands on the main rail, but at the rail's center junction, the left-facing gate directs it into a **side channel** that curves sharply left.
- It drops into the **Red Bin** (far left). **State unchanged**.

**Marble #3: Red into Basket 1**
- Drag another **Red marble** to **Basket 1**.
- Identical journey: spiral, pressure plate (gate stays left), down chute, onto main rail, forced left by diverter, into **Red Bin**.
- **Red Bin now contains 2 marbles**.

**Marble #4: Red into Basket 1**
- Drag third **Red marble** to **Basket 1**.
- Exact same path as above. Into **Red Bin**.
- **Red Bin now contains 3 marbles**.

**Marble #5: Yellow into Basket 1**
- Drag a **Yellow marble** to **Basket 1**.
- Spiral, pressure plate—**the diverter gate flips right** this time because it was hit from the opposite angle.
- It rolls down the chute, but now at the main rail junction, the **right-facing gate** blocks the left path and opens a **center channel**.
- The marble drops into the **Yellow Bin**.
- **Machine is now in State 2**: The left-side diverter gate points right, aligning the center channel for Basket 2's path.

**Marble #6: Red into Basket 1 (Final Basket 1 drop)**
- Drag final **Red marble** to **Basket 1**.
- Spiral, pressure plate flips gate **back to left**.
- Rolls down chute, main rail junction forces it left into **Red Bin**.
- **Red Bin now has all 4 Red marbles**. The left-side mechanism is now **locked** in the left position.

- It drops into the funnel and immediately lands in the **crossbow launcher**—a wooden arm cocked at 45 degrees.
- The arm swings forward with a *snap*, striking the marble and **launching it horizontally left** across a 5-inch gap.
- It lands on a **separate upper-right rail** with a *bonk*, rolls leftward, and hits a **button**—a brass nub that protrudes from the rail.
- The button **temporarily raises a blocker** in the center junction, preventing Basket 1 marbles from interfering (though none are coming now).
- The marble rolls off the rail's end, drops vertically two inches onto the **main horizontal rail** (now clear), and rolls right into the **Yellow Bin**.
- **Machine is now in State 3**: Center blocker is raised for 3 seconds, then automatically lowers.

- The channel curves smoothly, the marble rolls down a gentle slope, and drops into the **Blue Bin** (far right).

**Marble #9: Blue into Basket 2**
- Drag second **Blue marble** to **Basket 2**.
- Crossbow launch, upper-right rail, button hit (blocker raises), drops to main rail.
- The right-facing diverter again shunts it right into the channel, down the slope, into **Blue Bin**.
- **Blue Bin now contains 2 marbles**.

**Marble #10: Blue into Basket 2**
- Drag third **Blue marble** to **Basket 2**.
- Identical path: crossbow, button, right diverter, channel, slope, **Blue Bin**.
- **Blue Bin now contains 3 marbles**.

**Marble #11: Yellow into Basket 2**

- **Yellow Bin now contains 3 marbles**.

**Marble #12: Blue into Basket 2 (Final drop)**
- Drag final **Blue marble** to **Basket 2**.
- Crossbow launch, upper-right rail, button hit, drops to main rail.
- The right-facing diverter shunts it right into the channel, down the slope, and it lands in the **Blue Bin** with a definitive *clunk*.
- **Blue Bin now contains all 4 Blue marbles**.
