## What Each Marble Does (Mechanical Breakdown)

### Basket 1 Path (First 6 marbles)
When you drop marbles into the **left funnel**, they travel through:
1. **Initial spiral chute** - slows the marble and deposits it onto a main rail
2. **Toggle switches** - Each marble hits a pressure plate that flips a diverter gate
3. **Color-specific routing** - The path branches based on the marble's color and the current state of the gates
4. **Final approach** - Marbles are directed toward their matching bins (Red→Red Bin, Yellow→Yellow Bin)

**Specific behavior:**
- The **first Yellow** activates the primary pathway and sets the initial gate position
- The **three Reds** in sequence clear obstacles and tune the path for subsequent marbles
- The **second Yellow** verifies the path configuration
- The **final Red** completes the path setup for Basket 1

### Basket 2 Path (Last 6 marbles)
When you drop marbles into the **right funnel**, they encounter:
1. **Crossbow launcher** - Shoots the marble across to a separate rail network
2. **Button triggers** - Each marble depresses a button that temporarily opens/closes pathways
3. **Shared junction** - This path briefly intersects with Basket 1's path, requiring careful sequencing
4. **Color sorting** - The final diverters route Blue marbles to Blue Bin and Yellow to Yellow Bin

**Specific behavior:**
- The **Yellow** drop (#7) activates the Basket 2 mechanism and synchronizes it with Basket 1's state
- The **three Blues** (drops #8-10) each hit reset switches that prevent path conflicts
- The **final Yellow** (#11) triggers a release mechanism
- The **last Blue** (#12) travels the fully-cleared path to its destination

## Goal Completion
When all 12 marbles reach their **correctly colored bins**:
- All Red marbles land in the **Red Bin**
- All Yellow marbles land in the **Yellow Bin**
- All Blue marbles land in the **Blue Bin**
- **0 marbles are destroyed** in the optimal solution
- All 12 marbles are **returned to your inventory** for the next level
- You earn **11,590 points** (perfect efficiency score)

## Key Mechanics Demonstrated
This tutorial level teaches:
- **Path visualization:** Marbles physically alter the contraption as they travel
- **State changes:** Each marble flips switches that affect the next marble's route
- **Color matching:** Only correct-color marbles survive; wrong paths lead to destruction
- **Conservation:** No marbles are wasted in the optimal solution, though later levels require sacrificing marbles to trigger mechanisms

**Note:** If you deviate from this sequence, marbles will be destroyed by falling into wrong-color bins, getting stuck in glue traps, or entering infinite loops. You can purchase replacement marbles using points, but this reduces your efficiency score.
